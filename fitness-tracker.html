The Importance of BMI & Diet for Health

Maintaining a healthy body weight and ensuring a balanced diet are key components of long-term health. Body Mass Index (BMI) is an essential metric that helps people understand if their weight is within a healthy range for their height.

Whether you're looking to manage your weight or improve your general health, understanding your BMI and following a well-designed diet plan can make a significant difference.
