Solutions for Eye Strain and Vision Problems

To prevent eye strain, consider these measures:

  • Follow the 20-20-20 rule: Look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds every 20 minutes.
  • Use proper lighting and reduce glare from screens.
  • Use artificial tears or a humidifier if you have dry eyes.
  • Schedule regular eye check-ups and consider using blue-light filters for screens.
