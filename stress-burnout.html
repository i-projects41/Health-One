Solutions for Stress and Burnout

To manage stress and burnout, consider the following:

  • Practice mindfulness and meditation to help calm your mind.
  • Set realistic and achievable goals for work tasks.
  • Take regular breaks and allow yourself time to rest and recharge.
  • Seek professional help such as counseling or therapy if needed.
  • Engage in physical activities like walking or yoga to reduce stress.
